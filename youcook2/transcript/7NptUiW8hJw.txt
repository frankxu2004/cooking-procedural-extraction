a recipe for a classic macaroni salad is a must have during the summer.
start by cooking one box of elbow macaroni, according to the package directions, drain rinse and cool then add to a large bowl.
now combine one cup of mayonnaise, two tablespoons of vinegar, one tablespoon of dijon mustard, one teaspoon of sugar one teaspoon of salt and a quarter teaspoon of ground, black pepper to the same bowl.
add one cup thinly sliced celery, one cup of chopped green and red peppers and a quarter cup of chopped red onions.
this is a classic side to having an outdoor barbecue, and it can be made in advance to avoid any last minute shuffle classic.
