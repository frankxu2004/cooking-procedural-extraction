cut the beef in to 1 inch cubes
mix tsp salt 2 tsp brown sugar tsp lemon zest tsp cumin powder tsp paprika  tsp pepper tsp coriander powder
add 1/3 cup olive oil 2 tsp of tomato paste 5 finely chopped garlic cloves to and whisk to combine
transfer cup of marinate mixture to ziplock bag and add the beef keep the leftover marinate mixture in the bowl
massage the marinate with hands
cut off 1 lbs summer squash and 1 onion into 1 inch pieces
cut 8 ounces of cremini mushrooms
add all the vegetables to the leftover mixture and toss them around to mix well until they are coated
heat the grill medium high
thread the marinated beef and vegetables on to 10  12 inches skewers separately
put the skewers on to the grill close the grill and cook turning the skewers every 2  3 minutes
take the beef skewers off when they are lightly charred allover
cover the skewers loosely with foils to keep them warm
continue to cook the vegetable until they are charred all over
