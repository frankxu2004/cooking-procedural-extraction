boil the potatoes until it is half done then peel the skin and grate
heat butter in a pan
add finely chopped onions into the melted butter and salt till it gets brown
put grated potatoes in a bowl and pour salted onions into it
add 1 tsp salt black pepper powder little crushed nutmeg and finely chopped parsley and mix all the ingredients well
grease a baking tray with butter or oil and put the mixture on it and spread evenly
spread some butter on top of the evenly spread potato mash and season with black pepper powder
bake it in a preheated oven for 10  15 minutes at 200 degree and serve
