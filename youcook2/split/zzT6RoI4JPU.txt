chop onions equivalent to the quantity of beef meat
heat some oil in a deep pan and add chopped onions and fry till they turn brown
clean and chop 1 whole garlic
stir and let onions fry till they are completely brown
once the onions are done add garlic and stir
add the diced beef meat to it and roast it
add pepper cumin salt paprika red pepper powder stir everything and roast
add a cup of tomato puree and cook stirring
pour some warm water to it and cook until the meat becomes soft
melt some better in another small pan and add 3 tsp of flour to it and mix and roast until it browns
add the roasted flour to the cooking meat mixture and mix well and cook for 10 minutes more
finely chop marjoram and add it to the goulash
plate some bread pour the goulash garnish with some chopped onions and serve
