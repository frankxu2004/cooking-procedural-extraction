cut pancetta into cubes
boil water in a big deep pan add salt to it and dunk spaghetti in it and toss
put a little bit of olive oil in a pan add cubed pancetta and fry until crispy in medium heat
add ground black pepper to the fried pancetta and stir
add cooked spaghetti to the fried pancetta toss and mix well
add pasta water to it and stir further in low heat
put finely chopped parsley leaves in generous amount and stir
take 2 cracked eggs in a bowl add cheese a little bit of pepper and lightly whisk until they blend
turn off heat and add whisked mixture to cooked spaghetti and stir constantly adding pasta water to get creamy consistency
turn on the heat and cook lightly in a low heat to get the eggs cooked
