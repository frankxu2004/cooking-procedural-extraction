prepare chopped potato cauliflower carrot and bean
heat some oil in a pan and add saffron
add tomatoes and salt and press them to puree
add all the vegetables to the pan and mix
add chopped green chillies too
add more salt and biriyani masala to taste
add mint and coriander leaves
cover to cook until 70% cooked
add the remaining vegetable and cover to cook again
put the mixture and some steamed rice layer by layer with yogurt and oil in a baking tray
cook it in the oven and serve
