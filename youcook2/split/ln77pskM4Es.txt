put some drained soaked chickpeas in a food processor and add some garlic oil
add chopped onion parsley 1/2 spoon baking powder coriander and sea salt
add some sauce of your favourite
blend everything in the food processor
take the falafel paste out in a bowl
add some gluten free flour and mix it together
shape it into small balls by hand and fry on a baking tray with oil
