mix flour and water and knead into a dough
add minced garlic ginger bok choy green onions sesame oil salt pepper and sugar to ground beef
add soy sauce and rice vinegar and stir
cut the dough into medium size pieces
roll them using a rolling pin into circular shape
stuff each slice with the meat mixture and fold and seal it well
add oil to the pan and steam the potstickers
drizzle some oil and sprinkle spring onions on top
