remove 2 or 3 outer layers of cabbage and slice it in to thin pieces
put the sliced cabbage in a pot and sprinkle salt on top
add grated carrots finely chopped green chilies and grated garlic salt and mix
mash the vegetables with wood pounder
transfer the mixed vegetables to a clean mason jar little by little pressing the vegetable down using pounder
fill the top with cabbage leaves
place the lid on the jar
