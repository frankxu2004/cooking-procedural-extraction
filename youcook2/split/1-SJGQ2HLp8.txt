add flour and salt to a bowl
add yeast to water then add to flour and mix
take the dough out and mix with flour
pull the dough and fold it repeatedly to knead
cut the dough into fourths and roll
place the dough on a pan with flour and warm cloth on top and let sit
stretch out and press the dough
spread tomato paste over the dough
drizzle olive oil on top and sprinkle with salt
add grated parmesan and pieces of mozzarella cheese on top
place basil leaves and olive oil on top
move pizza to paddle and cook in oven
