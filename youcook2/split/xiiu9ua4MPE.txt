grate potatos and put in bowl
grate onion and add to potatos
squeeze moisture out of potatos and onion
crack 3 eggs into bowl and pour over potatos
add salt and pepper
add 1/3 cup of flour and mix
form mix into cake and drop into hot oil
cook on each side for 2 or 3 minutes
remove and drain on paper towel
place on plate with sour cream and chives
