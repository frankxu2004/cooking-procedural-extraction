clean and rinse canned escargots and pat them dry and later wash the hands
mash some softened butter in a bowl and add 2-3 minced garlic to it
add 1  tbsp of finely chopped chives little salt and white pepper
mix all the ingredients well by adding little bit of sherry cooking wine until the mixture reaches proper consistency
put small dollop of the mixture into the escargot baking dish
place one escargot over each of the dollops of mixture and pop them in
take the rest of the butter mixture and put them on top of escargots
place the dishes on a cooking sheet and put them in the preheated oven at 350-degree c for 15-18 minutes
remove the cooked escargots and serve them in the escargot baking dish itself
