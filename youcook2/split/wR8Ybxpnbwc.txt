add sushi rice to a pot and pour water
heat the pot on a stove until the water boils
mince the tuna and add it to a bowl
add sriracha sauce to the bowl
add sesame oil and thinly sliced scallions to the bowl and mix
spread the cooked rice on the sheet of nori shiny side down
sprinkle sesame seeds over the rice
flip the nori over and add the tuna mixture to the other side
roll the bamboo sheet over the nori while adding pressure
cut the roll in even slices
mix japanese mayo with sriracha sauce and add to the sushi pieces
