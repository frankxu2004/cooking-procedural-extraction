heat butter in a pan add cumin seeds black lentil red kidney beans water and pressure cook
add tomato puree  3 tomatoes and 3-4 cloves garlic grinded into puree  keep the flame medium and keep stirring
add coriander powder red chili powder and mix properly and cook stirring in between
mix masala tikka and water and pour into the dal add some butter and cook
take 1 tbsp of dry fenugreek leaves crush between palms and add it to the dish and mix
close the lid of the cooker and pressure cook the dal till further 2 whistles
add a pinch of nutmeg and milk mix and then simmer
pour the dal in a cup garnish with cream and kasuri methi on top and serve with chapatti or rice
