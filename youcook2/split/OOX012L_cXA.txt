spray olive oil into a glass pan
put chicken breast to the pan
add half cup of tomato sauce quarter tea spoon of oregano and minced garlic in a bowl and mix well
pour the mixture over the chicken breast in the pan
preheat the oven to 400 degrees
place the pan with ingredients in the oven and bake for 15 to 25 minutes
boil blanch spinach for a minute and pour it out in to a bowl
put the baked chicken over the boiled spinach and serve
