heat oil in a deep fryer to 365 degrees f
rinse fish pat dry and season with salt and pepper
combine flour garlic powder paprika 2 teaspoons salt and 2 teaspoons pepper
stir egg into the dry ingredients
gradually mix in beer until a thin batter is formed
dip fish fillets into the batter then drop one at a time into hot oil
fry fish turning once until both sides are golden brown
drain on paper towels and serve warm
