take 2 cups of garbanzo beans and 1 1/2 cup of fava beans and soak it in water overnight
blend chickpeas and fava beans seperately without water and put it in a big bowl
blend yellow onion 2 cups cilantro 1 cup parsley 2 tbls minced garlic to smooth paste add to beans mixture
add 1/2 cup breadcrumbs 2 tsp salt 1 tbls coriander 1 tps cumin 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper mix
add 1 tbls of water if the mixture is too dry
fry the falafel balls in oil and fry until it become dark golden brown
