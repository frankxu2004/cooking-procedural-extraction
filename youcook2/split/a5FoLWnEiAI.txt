soak some black lentils and red kidney beans in water
cook them in a pot of water with lid on
blend the lentil and red kidney beans in the pot
heat a pan add butter oil cumin seeds coriander seeds and onion and stir
cover with a lid and stir
add chopped tomatoes mix it well and cover
mix dal makhni masala red chili powder and turmeric powder in a small bowl of water
pour the sauce in the pan and add the lentil and red kidney beans to the pot and mix them well
add some salt
top the dal with cream and cilantro
