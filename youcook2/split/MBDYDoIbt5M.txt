peel carrots and cut off the tops
trim into the length of a hotdog
boil in water
add soy sauce water vinegar miso paste to a seal bag
add liquid smoke syrup salt garlic powder onion powder and smoked paprika
take the carrots out cool under cold water and put into the seal bag
cook the carrots on a pan and pour some juice on the pan
put the carrot rod in hotdog buns and serve with vegetable and sauce
