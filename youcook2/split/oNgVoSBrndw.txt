combine chopped parsley cilantro and onion in the food processor and mix well until the ingredients are finely chopped
add some cloves of garlic and overnight soaked chickpeas to food processor and blend all ingredients to form smooth paste
add 1 tsp cumin 1 tsp paprika 1 tsp black pepper half tsp coriander 3/2 tsp salt to paste
mix the ingredients in the bowl
put some oil in a pot and heat it to 375 degrees
make small falafel balls from the paste and fry the balls in the oil until cooked completely
apply tahini sauce spicy sauce to pita bread and add falafel tomatoes lettuce cucumber and pickles to the pita bread
