add 1 tbsp oil to a heated pan and 1 tbsp onion and cook on low flame
add 1 tsp of mustard seeds 1 tsp of cumin seeds few curry leaves and saute
add  chopped tomato 1 tsp red chilli powder and cook for 2 min
now add the boiled channa into the pan with rest of the ingredients and mix well
add 2 tbsp tamarind extract and mix
add 1 tsp of salt 1 tsp of sugar and cook for 10 min
serve it in a bowl garnished with chopped onion and coriander leaves
