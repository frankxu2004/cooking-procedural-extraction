in pan take 1 pound ground beef add 1 finely chopped onions 2 cloves finely minced garlic cook 5 mins
now add 2 tbls of chili powder 1 tbls of cumin powder and cook for 3 minutes
add 1 cup of water and 6 ounce of canned tomato paste blend it well and season it with salt
 add 1 tbls prepared yellow mustard 1 tbls worcestershire sauce 2 tsp brown sugar mix simmer 5 minutes
steam frankfurters for 4 minutes in same steamer place the hot dog buns and steam for a couple of minutes
place frankfurter in middle of bun cover sausage with chili prepared earlier top with mustard sauce garnish with chopped onions
