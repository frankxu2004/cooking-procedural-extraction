heat some vegetable oil in a pan add minced garlic and fry add chopped zucchini and carrots and saute
then add 1 tbsp of chopped spring onions and saute that with vegetables
next add shirataki noodles  rinsed under the tap of hot water  and fry everything for few minutes
add baobab powder gluten-free tamari and agave and mix everything until the sauce is properly coated
after the sauce is reduced and coated add textured vegetable protein and mix well and fry further
then add bean sprout and fry all together season with black pepper and fry everything until the sauce is completely reduced
pour it on a plate garnish with chopped roasted peanuts and serve
