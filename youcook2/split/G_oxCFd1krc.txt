chop some tomatoes to halves and put on a baking pan
chop some white onions to chunks and add to the tomato
smash 3 cloves of garlic and mix with the onions
spread some olive oil salt and pepper to the baking pan
roast the ingredients for 40 minutes
put the roasted ingredients in a soup pot
add 4 cups of chicken broth to the pot
heat the soup to a boil
simmer for 25 minutes and use a blender to blend everything in the soup
add more salt and pepper to the soup and stir
take out the soup and garnish with basil leaves and olive oil to serve
