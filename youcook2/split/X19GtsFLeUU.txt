add buttermilk chilli powder and cayenne pepper to a bowl and stir
take 15 chicken wings on a plate add salt to taste
pour the marinate mixture on top of the wings mix it and refridgerate
dredge the chicken wings in the bowl of flour to cover it
deep fry the flour coated wings in oil at 350 degrees for 3-5 mins
place the wings in bowl pour buffalo hot sauce on them toss and serve with celery sticks
