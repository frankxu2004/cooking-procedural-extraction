peel and cut the potatoes
add oil and cumin seeds to a pan
add the peas to the pan
add ginger salt chili chili powder garam masala and coriander to the potatoes
mix the flour oil and salt
mix in water to form a dough
knead the dough until soft
roll a ball out into a disk
slice the disk in half
spread the paste on the cut edge
form a cone with the dough
fill the cone with the filling
seal the edges of the cone
cook the samosa in oil
