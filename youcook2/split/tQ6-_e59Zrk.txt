finely chop small head of cabbage and put it in a bowl
grate 2 medium to large carrots
add grated carrots to the cabbage and 1 tbsp of himalayan pink salt
add 1 tsp of fennel or kummel seeds and 1 tbsp of organic coconut sugar
massage all the ingredients together and mix
transfer the mixture to another bowl press it to the bottom and stick it
take a plate and put it on top of the mixture and place something heavy on top
cover it up with cling film and let ferment
