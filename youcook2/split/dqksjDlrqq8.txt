preheat the pan and add little bit of olive oil and heat it
add 1  lb of minced pork break it up and cook
chop 4 cloves of garlic and add them to the cooking pork and mix breaking the meat into small pieces
add nice drizzle of sage dried thyme paprika freshly ground pepper and salt and cook stirring it till the meat turns brown
once the pork is browned add 1 finely chopped red bell pepper in one corner of the pan and fry
finely chop 1 bowl full of parsley
add the chopped parsley to the cooking ingredients in the pan and salt
make space on one part of the pan and add grated cauliflower and fry and cook for 5 minutes mixing all together
transfer the cooked dish to a serving bowl and serve
