add flour sugar oil and yeast to a bowl
add salt plain yogurt and water and mix to form a dough
take the dough in a separate bowl and let it aside for 30-40 minutes in a warm place
grease the dough dip it in flour and roll it into a round shape
heat a pan and cook the naan on medium heat for 2-3 minutes with covered lid
flip it over the flame to let it puff and flip it until it's done
spread butter over the naan and sprinkle some kasuri methi
