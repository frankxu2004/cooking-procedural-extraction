break down the napa cabbage into small pieces
sprinkle salt on the cabbage
add two tsp of sweet rice flour into the water heat it and whisk it until it is thick and let it cool
add cut onions ginger garlic and pealed asian pear and wet it all with fish sauce and grind it
add red pepper flakes and grind paste into the cold flour paste
mix it all until well blended
turn over the cabbage leaves every 30 minutes
add the cut carrot green onions korean cabbage and paste in batches into the cabbage bowl
toss the mixture around to coat the paste evenly and store them in glass jar
