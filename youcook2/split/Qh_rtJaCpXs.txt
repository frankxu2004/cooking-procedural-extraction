 1 clove garlic paste black pepper powder salt grated parmesan cheese 1 egg chopped parsley chopped red pepper to chicken
add a pour of bread crumbs and massage using hand to bind the mixture together
put olive oil in a frying pan and add the ready meat balls and fry
to make sauce  take some olive oil in deep pan and add finely chopped onions carrots and celery and fry
to this further add a can of chopped tomatoes and hot water and stir well and bring it to boil
add some salt and chopped basil leaves and cook for 35 minutes
now add the fried meatballs to the sauce and mix
