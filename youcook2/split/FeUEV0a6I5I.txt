add 4 chopped tomatoes with 1 cup water in a pan
add the bayleaf peppercorns and mix well
cook on a medium flame for 8 to 10 minutes while stirring occasionally
remove the bayleaf and let the mixture cool completely
blend the mixture in a mixer till smooth and strain it using a strainer
heat some butter in an empty pan add the plain flour and saute on medium flame for 1 minute
add the prepared tomato mixture 1 cup of water tomato puree
add sugar salt pepper powder and fresh cream
mix well and cook on a medium flame for 2 minutes while stirring occasionally
garnish with fresh cream and serve hot with bread croutons
