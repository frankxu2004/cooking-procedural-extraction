in a hot pot add butter flour whole milk and kosher salt
simmer the mixture and add grated parmesan cheese and gruyere cheese and cook on low heat
put butter on the grill and toast slices of sourdough bread and black forest ham
add more gruyere cheese on the ham and apply dijon mustard on the bread
put the ham on the bread and pour some bechamel sauce on the bread
put the sandwich in the oven to toast the cheese
