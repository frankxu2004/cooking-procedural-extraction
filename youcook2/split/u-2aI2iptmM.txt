pour some oil on the pan on a medium heat
scramble one egg on the pan and pour the egg in a container
pour oil on the pan and add onions garlic brown sugar and sea salt
cook the mixture for 5 minutes till the onion becomes light brown in color
add precooked jasmine thai kitchen rice
add some peppers peas tofu and cooked shrimp
stir the mixture and add fish sauce and chilli sauce as per taste
stir fry the mixture for 3 minutes till the rice gets cooked completely
add scrambled egg on the rice and mix it well with the rice
