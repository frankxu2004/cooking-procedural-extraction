add flour to a bowl and add water and mix
knead the dough well and make into a sphere
add oil to the dough and let the dough rest
take a pinch of the dough and roll into a circle
pat the dough onto dry flour and roll into a thin circle
flip the dough between your hands to get rid of excess flour
place the dough onto a pan over heat
flip the roti to the other side
remove the roti from the pan and place directly on the fire
