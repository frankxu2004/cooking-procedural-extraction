melt 1 spoon butter and cook some chopped onions in a pan
add the cooked garlic and some ground meat in a bowl
add beaten egg and some salt with chopped green onions
add some barbecue sauce and tomato sauce to the mixture
add some soy sauce and milk
prepare some buttered bread crumbs and add to the mixture
mix everything together by hand
fill a baking pan with the mixture
prepare some sauce by mixing ketchup and bbq sauce
spread a little mixed sauce on top of the meat and cook in oven for 30 minutes
take it out  spread the rest of the sauce and cook for another 30 minutes
season the meat with chopped parsley leaves
