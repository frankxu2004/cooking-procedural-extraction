slice the onions into thick slices and separate the rings

crack one egg in another bowl and beat it and add in the milk and mix it together
coat the onion rings in flour mix

dip the onion rings in the batter and coat it with the panko breadcrumbs and place it on a wire rack
fry the onion rings in hot oil on medium heat until golden brown on both side
