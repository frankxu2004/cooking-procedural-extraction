preheat oven to 350 f degree
chop one large onion into pieces
cut some slices of white bread into cubes
add the chopped onions to some meat loaf
season with salt black pepper and garlic powder and mix them together
add the cubed bread to the mixture
pour some milk and one egg and mix them together
add some yellow mustard and some ketchup
mix the mixture and place into the pan
add a little yellow mustard ketchup and brown sugar and stir to combine
pour and spread the sauce
bake the mixture in the oven
