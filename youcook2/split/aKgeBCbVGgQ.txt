peel a potato and remove any dark spots
place the potatoes into a pot of boiling water
remove the potatoes from the water and grate into thin strips
add flour and egg to the potatoes
mix the potato flour and egg mixture and mix into a dough
roll the dough into a ball
place the dough balls into a pot of boiling water
