peel seven large red potatoes
boil potatoes
chop two large onions
melt one stick of butter and sautee onions
save two cups of water from cooked potatoes
put onions in a large bowl and add half a pound of grated cheese
mash potatoes into onion and cheese bowl and add salt and pepper
add four cups of flour two table spoons of butter and two egg yokes to mixing bowl
add two table spoons of sour cream
add saved water from potatoes and mix with fork
cover dough with paper towel for ten minutes
roll out dough to 1/8 inch thickness
cut three inch circles and fill with potato onion and cheese mixture
pinch shut and drop into boiling water for 2 minutes
