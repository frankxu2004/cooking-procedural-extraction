heat wok
add oil red pepper carrots bamboo cabbage and bean sprouts to wok
add noodles oyster sauce soy sauce sugar salt and pepper to wok
take off heat and transfer to a bowl to cool
put pan on medium heat and add frying oil
place spring roll wrapper down
brush edges of wrapper with egg
place filling on the wrapper
lift wrapper over top and tuck it under the filling
fold left side of wrapper over the top and then the right side then roll
bush a little bit of egg on the top and seal roll
put rolls into hot oil
remove roll from oil and put on a towel to drain
