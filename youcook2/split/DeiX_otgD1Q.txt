spread rock salt on a baking tray place potatoes on it and draw few spikes
pierce the knife inside the potatoes and find if the potatoes are cooked properly
cut the cooked potatoes in half and scoop the flesh and put it in a bowl
mash the potatoes using masher or the potato ricer
mix both the types of mashed potatoes together
keep the mashed potatoes on the flame and mix adding butter until it is mix well and the bottom of the pan becomes shiny
add 400 ml of warm milk and incorporate to make the potato creamier
add little salt and pepper and keep stirring
tear the calvinator leaves roughly and add them to the potatoes mix and cook in steam
add chopped spring onions and give a stir add little more warm milk to keep the dish creamier
add chopped parsley and stir
finish the dish by adding more spring onions parsley and a little bit of olive oil at the end and serve
