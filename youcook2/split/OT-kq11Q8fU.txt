crack the eggs and whisk them
add flour and beer and mix well
add some black pepper and fine salt and mix it all up in to a batter
add little water if the batter is thick to bring it to proper consistency
cut the calamari in to little rolls
put the cut calamari into the batter and marinate
fry the marinated calamari in oil
take out the fried calamari and put them on a paper to take off excessive oil
sprinkle some salt over calamari
slice a lemon and serve calamari with it
