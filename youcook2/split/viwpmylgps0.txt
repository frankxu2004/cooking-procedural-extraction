slice 2 kilos of tomatoes in half and place it flesh side up on a baking tray
cut two spanish onions into quarter pieces and scatter them on the tomatoes
squash about 7-8 cloves of garlic and distribute it around the onions and tomatoes
season with some sea salt cracked pepper and dried oregano add some extra virgin olive oil around the pan
cook it in the oven
pop all the garlic flesh out and discard the skin
pour all the ingredients into a soup pot pour the stock into the pot and put it on medium heat
add some chopped basil leaves into it
tear half ciabatta bread pieces into the pot and stir
slice and toast the bread in the pan
season the sauce with a splash of red wine vinegar
blend the soup and add olive oil
