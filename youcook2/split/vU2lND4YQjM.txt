clean the prawns leaving the tail piece and make 3 small slits on each prawn
take the ready shrimps in a bowl and add 2 tbsp of fish sauce 1 tsp of soy sauce 1 tsp of thick teriyaki sauce  - 1 tsp of sesame oil and 3 cloves of minced garlic mix them all and marinate for 2 hours
to make tempura batter mix 100 gms of tempura mix flour with 160 ml of water and whisk
take marinated shrimp by tail dip it in batter and coat with bread crumbs and deep fry till golden brown
remove the cooked shrimps and drain excess oil using paper towel and serve
