cut 1 white onion into circular slices and remove the layers of onion
add tepung terigu flour tepung maizena baking soda garam secukupnya paprika bubu bubuk and bawang and whisk together
add sliced onions to it and coat them well with the flour mixture and set aside
pour  gelas air soda dingin to remaining flour mixture in the bowl and whisk to form a thick batter
dip each coated onion into the batter and deep fry in batches
once the onion rings are golden brown remove from oil and drain the excess oil
