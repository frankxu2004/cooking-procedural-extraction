mix yeast water and sugar in a cup
mix flour oil salt and the yeast mixture together
form the dough into a ball and cover
knead the dough and cut into pieces
form each piece into a ball roll out and roll up
place on a pan and cover with a towel
bake the bread in an oven
slice the bun and open it up
spread the bun with mustard
place cucumber salad and then the hot dog on the bun
drizzle ketchup and fried onions on top
