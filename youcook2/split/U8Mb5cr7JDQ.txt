remove the outer leaves and the stem of a cabbage
weigh the cabbage and prepare salt of 2% of its weight
cut the cabbage into slices
add the salt to the cabbage in a large bowl and massage the cabbage
place the cabbage to a jar and press it firmly to the bottom
instead of cutting cabbage you can also use a grinder to process the cabbage
seal the jar and put it in room temperature for 3 days
add some water to the jar to seal and put in a cool place
