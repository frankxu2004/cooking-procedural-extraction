put maida in a deep bowl add sooji baking soda salt and mix well
add yogurt and mix well
add water and start to knead in to stiff dough
once dough is ready cover and keep it to rest for 4 hours
heat up oil add cumin seeds onion and cook until onion gets golden brown
add ginger garlic paste and cook
add chana masala and cook for 2 minutes
add tomatoes red chilli powder mix cover and cook for 5 minutes
add soaked chana salt water cover and pressure cook
after 4 hours divide dough in small balls
deep fry until gets golden color from both sides
