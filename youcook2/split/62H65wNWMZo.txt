heat some oil in a pan and cook minced beef
add some chopped carrots and onions and cook
add the beef water and a stock cube and simmer
add some tomato ketchup and worcestershire sauce and stir
add some milk mustard and some grated cheese and mash
mix cornstarch with water and pour it into the gravy
once the gravy thickens take it off the heat and pour it to a baking dish
spread the mashed potatoes over the gravy evenly and scour using a fork
sprinkle rest of the cheese on the top and place the dish in the oven
