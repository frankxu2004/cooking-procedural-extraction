cut the onions into thin slices
add butter to hot pan little veggie oil 1  tbsp brown sugar and place onion slices on pan to caramelize
add wine to the pan to cover onions and wrap pan with aluminium foil and let cook 5-6 min
oil the sausages and place them on a griddle pan
add little thyme and grated nutmeg
grate little orange zest into the pan
slice open the buns and place the sausages between them
now put the caramelized onions between the buns squeeze mustard over it to serve
