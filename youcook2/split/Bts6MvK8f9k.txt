heat oil on a pan and add cashews
add mustard seeds cumin and chana dal to the pan
add the onion and curry leaves to the pan
add chopped green chilis and ginger to the pan
add turmeric powder asafetida and boiled potatoes to the pan
add salt and the cashew nuts to the pan
add chopped coriander to the potatoes
spread oil over a hot pan
spread dosa batter on the pan in a circle
add the potato masala in the middle of the dosa
fold one half of the dosa over the potato
remove the dosa from the pan and serve on a plate
